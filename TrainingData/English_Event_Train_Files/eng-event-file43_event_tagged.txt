 The accident took place when a truck rammed into another resulting in the latter colliding with a bus coming from the opposite direction. 

 The bus was carrying 42 pilgrims, who were on their way back to Yelamanchili and Narsipatnam after visiting Varanasi. 

 They were returning after visiting Arasavilli and Srikurmam shrines in Srikakulam district when the mishap happened. 

 The deceased — Karanam Venkanna (60), Bysetty Atchyuthamma (55) and Kalagada Appala Narasamma (60) — hailed from Yelamanchili and neighbouring Narsipatnam.

 The death count is likely to go up as two of the injured are in critical condition. The police further added that the truck drive responsible for the initial collision had fled the scene.
